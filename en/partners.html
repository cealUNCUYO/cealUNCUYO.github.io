Relationships with other institutions
   
CEAL has relationships with several education and R&D institutions, within Argentina as well as in other countries.
  
Common activities with these institutions are from several types, including human resources formation activities, and also joint research and development projects.

With respect to human resources formation, an important number of exchanges of professors among the related institutions is carried out. Professors from the partners institutions are received in the Master in Logistics program as well as in the Industrial Engineering program of the School of Engineering of the UNCuyo on a regular basis. Besides, professors and researchers from CEAL give courses and seminars on some of these institutions.

From the research and development point of view, joint projects are carried out, with different kinds of financial resources (for mor details see the Research section).
 
The main institutions CEAL has relationships with are:
  • Institut National Polytechnique de Lorraine (Nancy, France)
  • Supply Chain & Logistics Institute (GeorgiaTech, USA)
  • École Nationale d’Ingénieurs de Metz (ENIM, Metz, France)
  • Universidade Federal do Paraná (UFPR, Brazil)
  • Universidad de Santiago de Chile (USACH, Chile) 
  • Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (PUC, Chile)
  • Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso (PUCV, Chile)
  • Universidad Politécnica de Valencia (UPV, Valencia, España)
  • Universidad de Boyacá (Colombia)
  • Universidad Tecnológica Nacional (Facultad Regional Mendoza, Argentina)
  • Universidad Nacional de San Juan (San Juan, Argentina)
  • Universidad Nacional del Centro de la Provincia de Buenos Aires (Argentina)

Additionally, CEAL is part of some research networks and councils. The most important of these councils is the Wine Supply Chain Council, which is composed of several institutions, namely the GeorgiaTech (USA), the Catholic University of Chile (Chile), the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR, South Africa) and  Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (CSIRO, Australia).

The other network from which CEAL is a member is the International Network on New Party Logistics(IN-NPL). This is an international network of researchers and professionals on logistics, on which also participate members of several universities and laboratories, such us l'Université de Nice Sophia Antipolis (France), la Università Carlo Cattaneo (Italy), the Henri Tudor Research Centre (Luxembourg) and the Universidade Federal do Paraná (Brazil).
