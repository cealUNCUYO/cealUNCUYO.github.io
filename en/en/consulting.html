Consulting
   
Our vision at CEAL is that globalization will force industrial enterprises to innovate and optimize their organizations, processes and functions related to logistics and production activities, in order to achieve a world class level.
 
Because of this, one of our main objectives at CEAL is to satisfy enterprises requirements by means of specialized profesional services and consulting, from the R+D+I perspective, focused on management, logistics and production processes.
 
Human Resources
 
Given the multidisciplinary approach of all projects carried out, CEAL if composed of professionals and researchers from several disciplines:
