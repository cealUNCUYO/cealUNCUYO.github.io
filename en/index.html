Welcome to the Logistics Studies and Applications Centre (CEAL)
The Logistics Studies and Applications Centre (CEAL, Centro de Estudios y Aplicaciones Logísticas) is a Reseach, Development and Innovation Centre (R+D+I), related to the School of Engineering of the National University of Cuyo (UNCuyo) in Mendoza, Argentina.
 
This R&D centre was born in 1998, in order to satisfy the need of highly
skilled professional consulting, and also for developing academic reseach and development on the west region of Argentina, with special emphasis on the application of technology on industrial domains.
 
Activities at CEAL are focused on Logistics, Supply Chain Management and Industrial Management and Optimization domains. All projects carried out at CEAL follow a multidisciplinary approach, focusing on the development and transference of technology.
 
CEAL is also committed to human resources formation, being tightly related to the Masters in Logistics and the Industrial Engineering programs of the School of Engineering of UNCuyo.
